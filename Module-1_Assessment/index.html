About Me

My name is Antonique Tyler and I have a variety of hobbies I enjoy. My favorite hobby is coding and learning new programming languages. The fun thing about coding is problem solving and constructing new things, it's been a great creative outlet and a fulfilling experience thus far!

When I'm not coding, I love traveling. I've been to a number of European countries, and my favorite city is Paris. It's a beautiful and elegant place, with delicious food, and diverse groups of people.

Another hobby of mine is going to museums. Whenever I get the chance, I try to visit museums around town and in different countries. My favorite museum is actually in Paris, and it's the Louvre!
